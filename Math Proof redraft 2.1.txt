These assumptions are minimal and non-physical.

\rho_{n+1} = L(\rho_n), \quad

4. CHSH Consistency Test

Using the Bell state |\Phi^+\rangle and standard measurement settings:
	•	Alice: A_0 = Z, \; A_1 = X
	•	Bob: B_0 = \frac{Z+X}{\sqrt{2}}, \; B_1 = \frac{Z-X}{\sqrt{2}}

The CHSH functional:

S = E(A_0,B_0) + E(A_0,B_1) + E(A_1,B_0) - E(A_1,B_1)
